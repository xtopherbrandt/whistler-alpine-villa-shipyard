# Shareholder Portal

## Description

Each shareholder's personal view of the system. Shareholders can manage check-ins and check-outs for their own units, register guest stays (friends or family), and view the occupancy status of the complex (which units are occupied — not by whom or why). Shareholders with multiple shares see all their units in one place.

## Business Value

Gives shareholders a self-service tool to manage their unit usage without contacting the caretaker. The occupancy overview lets shareholders know when the complex is busy — useful for planning their own visits.

## Dependencies

Requires E001 (Occupancy & Check-In) — check-in/out functionality is shared.
Requires E005 (User & Access Management) — shareholder accounts must exist.
