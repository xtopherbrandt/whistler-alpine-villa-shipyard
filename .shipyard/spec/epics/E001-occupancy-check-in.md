# Occupancy & Check-In

## Description

The digital replacement for the paper notebook. Shareholders register their stays by entering check-in dates, check-out dates, and the vehicles they are bringing. When a shareholder delegates their unit to friends or family, they register that stay on their behalf — the caretaker and directors are automatically notified.

The system tracks current and upcoming occupancy across all 52 units in 7 buildings.

## Business Value

Eliminates the shared paper notebook as the occupancy record. Gives the caretaker and directors an accurate, always-current view of who is expected in the complex and which vehicles are authorized in the parking lot. Streamlines the friend/family delegation process that currently requires manual emails to caretaker and directors.

## Dependencies

Requires E005 (User & Access Management) — shareholder accounts must exist before check-ins can be recorded.
