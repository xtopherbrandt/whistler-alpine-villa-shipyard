# Reporting & Statistics

## Description

Usage statistics for two audiences:

- **Directors & caretaker:** Complex-wide utilization — nights per unit per year, peak occupancy periods, guest-use vs owner-use breakdown across the complex.
- **Shareholders:** Their own usage summary — nights per unit per year across their share(s), owner vs guest-use breakdown for their unit(s).

## Business Value

Gives directors the data to understand how the complex is being used and identify trends. Gives shareholders visibility into their own utilization. Enables evidence-based decisions about complex management.

## Dependencies

Requires E001 (Occupancy & Check-In) — statistics are derived from check-in history.
Requires E005 (User & Access Management) — roles determine which statistics each user can see.
